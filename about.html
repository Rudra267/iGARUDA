About Us

Cybersecurity Redefined – The iGARUDAPromise

In today’s digital-first world, cybersecurity is not an option—it’s a necessity. iGARUDAis a next-generation cybersecurity firm specializing in application security, vulnerability assessments, penetration testing (VAPT), compliance audits, and threat intelligence. We go beyond traditional security measures to offer real-world attack simulations, proactive monitoring, and risk mitigation strategies that safeguard businesses against emerging cyber threats.

Our Mission

To empower businesses with cutting-edge cybersecurity solutions that ensure digital resilience, compliance, and proactive risk management.

Our Vision

To become a global leader in cybersecurity, providing organizations advanced solutions that protect against evolving threats.

Our Services

What We Do

1. Cyber Risk Assessment & VAPT

Our certified white-hat hackers conduct thorough Vulnerability Assessment & Penetration Testing (VAPT) to expose and remediate security loopholes. We simulate real-world cyberattacks to test your applications, networks, and cloud infrastructure, ensuring they are resilient against threats.

Read More

2. Threat Intelligence & Risk Management

We don't just identify threats—we neutralize them before they become a risk. Our continuous threat intelligence monitoring detects security vulnerabilities, phishing attacks, and social engineering scams, helping organizations stay one step ahead of cybercriminals.

Read More

3. Cybersecurity Consulting & Advisory

Security isn't a one-time fix—it's an ongoing process. We provide security consulting to help businesses integrate security best practices into DevOps. Our expertise ensures that your applications, cloud environments, and IT infrastructure are fortified against cyber threats.

Read More

4. Security Audits & Compliance

Regulatory compliance is key to business integrity. We assist organizations in adhering to ISO 27001, HIPAA, GDPR, DPDP Act, and other global security frameworks by conducting in-depth audits and risk assessments. Our compliance strategies help mitigate legal and financial risks while strengthening your security posture.

Read More

5. Phishing Simulations & Employee Awareness Training

Human error remains one of the biggest cybersecurity risks. Our real-time phishing simulations and cybersecurity training programs help businesses educate employees on identifying and preventing cyber threats. We offer both short-term and long-term training programs tailored to your industry.

Read More

6. Managed Security Operations (SIEM & SOC)

Our Security Information and Event Management (SIEM) and Security Operations Center (SOC) solutions provide 24/7 real-time monitoring, incident detection, and rapid response. We leverage AI-powered analytics and threat intelligence to safeguard businesses from cyberattacks.
